Finding the Perfect Place to Stay: Beyond Hotels

Explore alternative accommodation options that can save you money and provide more authentic travel experiences than traditional hotels.

1 Plan the budget Find the accommodation within the budget.
2 Considering the preferable type of accommodation Consider hotel if you prioritize in services and facilities. Consider Airbnb or homestay if you prefer a more comfort and undisturbed place to live.
3 Focus on accommodation's location Find accommodation that is near the public transport and shops.
4 Check the reviews and ask around Avoid accommodations with low rating, safety issues and cleanliness issues.
5 Check the facilities of the accommodation Consider hotel as an accommodation if you are looking for facilities and services like gym room, swimming pool and breakfast services.
