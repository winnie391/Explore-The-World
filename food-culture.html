How to Experience Local Cuisine Like a Pro

Tips for experiencing local cuisine and respecting cultural norms while traveling abroad. Learn how to find authentic food and navigate dining etiquette around the world.

1 Do research before travelling Learn about local culture. Be respectful to local beliefs and norms. Avoid any offensive actions that are impolite.
2 Do research about famous foods Know about what type fo food the place is famous about, only then find the restaurant that have these dishes.
3 Speak to locals Learn about what is their favourite dishes and receive recommended local dishes from them.
4 Avoid fast foods like burgers and pizzas Find restaurants that specialise only one or two types of foods. Normally, those foods are famous in local.
5 Attend cooking classes Learn to cook local dishes. Gain valuable memories as well as tasting local foods.
6 Visit food festivals and street vendors Lots of local foods are available in these festivals and the streets.
