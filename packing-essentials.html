Ultimate Packing Guide for Any Trip

Learn how to pack efficiently for any type of trip, from weekend getaways to long-term adventures. Our comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know.

Items in categories

Clothing

  • T-shirts
  • Blouses
  • Pants
  • Shorts
  • Skirts
  • Dresses
  • Jackets
  • Innerwear
  • Socks
  • Sleepwear
  • Swimsuits (Optional)

Accessories

  • Hats
  • Sunglasses
  • Glasses/Contact lens
  • Bags
  • Belts
  • Scarves
  • Jewellery
  • Watch

Shoes

Depends on the trips

  • Slippers (Beach vacation)
  • Sandals (Citywalk)
  • Sports shoes (Hiking)

Season

Items essential in winter

  • Sweathers/Hoodies
  • Winter jackets
  • Long coats
  • Beanies
  • Gloves
  • Earmuffs
  • Boots

Toiletries

  • Toothbrush
  • Toothpaste
  • Comb
  • Skincare products
  • Body wash
  • Shampoo & conditioner
  • Shaving kit
  • Perfume/Deodorant
  • Hair dryer
  • Nail clippers

Electronics

  • Smartphone
  • Smartphone charger
  • Adapter
  • Power bank
  • Camera

Documents

  • Identity card
  • Driver's license (Road trip)
  • Passport (Overseas trip)
  • Visa
  • Tickets
  • Travel reservations
  • Health insurance card
  • Travel insurance information

Others

  • Medicine
  • First aid kit
  • Vitamins
  • Cash
  • Credit card/Debit card
  • Luggage travel lock

Short-term trips

  • Travel light
  • Few clothing
  • Travel-size toiletries
  • Basic medications

Long-term adventures

  • More clothing (considering different season)
  • Bring enough toiletries
  • First aid kit
  • Portable clotheslines and detergent

Tips: It is recommended to buy items like clothes, toiletries, and detergent along your trip.
