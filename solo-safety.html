Essential Safety Tips for Solo Travelers

Stay safe while exploring the world on your own with these practical safety tips and precautions for solo travelers of all experience levels.

Research before you plan the trip

Choose destination wisely based on several conditions like safety issues, health issues, flight ticket and accommodation.

Gather information about the destination

Understand the culture and potential problems that may encounter at the destination.

Choose the accommodation carefully

Avoid accommodations located in remote areas.

Buy travel insurance

Provide benefits that compesate the losses due to flight cancelled, flight delayed and baggage lost.

Arrive at the destination during daylight

Easier to find transportation and safer.

Navigation skills

Learn to use GPS, identify direction based on road signs and sun's position.

Keep the important documents carefully

Store the important documents and extra cash in a separate pocket

First aid kit

Bring along necessary mecidine, pain killer, vitamins and bandages to prepare for injuries or illnesses

Be careful with strangers

Do not trust anyone during the trip

Do not show off your wealth

Wear simple clothes and do not wear expensive watch or jewellery.

Always refer to the map

To prevent taking the wrong path.

Bring a safety lock and baggage lock

Always lock the doors with safety locks and baggage with baggage locks.
